Achievement of our School

Our school's soccer team defied all the odds as they were crowned as the champion of this year's sports fest of the University Athletic Association of the Philippines. Their score is 1 in the first half and another 1 to the second over the three-time defending champion on saturday, August 19, at the Smokey Mountain (SM) in Manila.
